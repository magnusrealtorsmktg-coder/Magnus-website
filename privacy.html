Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Magnus Realtors® (“we”, “us” or “our”) collects, uses and protects the information you provide when you visit our website or contact us. By using this website, you agree to the practices described below.

Information we collect

We only collect information you choose to share with us, primarily through our enquiry, consultation and review forms. This may include:

  • Your name, phone number and email address;
  • Your property preferences (such as location, budget, requirement);
  • Any message, review or other details you submit voluntarily.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiries and arrange property visits or consultations;
  • To provide and improve our real-estate advisory services;

  • To contact you about properties or services relevant to your request.

How your information is shared

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We use trusted third-party services to operate this website — for example, a form-delivery service to send us your enquiries and a content-management service to publish website content. These providers process data only as needed to provide their service. We may also disclose information where required by law.

Cookies and analytics

This website may use essential and third-party scripts (such as web fonts) that can set cookies for basic functionality and performance. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Data retention and security

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to respond to your request and meet legal obligations, and we take reasonable measures to protect it against unauthorised access.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information you have provided. To do so, contact us using the details below.

Changes to this policy
